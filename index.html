• ~0.5 cups of flour
  • ~0.5 cube of margarine or butter
  • 3.5 cups of milk
  • 2 cans of tuna
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 0.25 cups of water
  1. Stir the flour and margarine (or butter) together in a saucepan over medium heat until they make a paste.
  2. Add the milk.
  3. Stir the mixture until it begins to thicken (~10 min).
  4. Add the tuna and continue stirring about 5 more minutes.
  5. In a separate container mix the cornstarch and water.
  6. Add the cornstarch-water combination to the mixture and stir.
  7. Serve over saltine crackers or rice.
